## What is Albumza?

Albumza is a **single-currency** social-gaming platform built explicitly to operate in states where the dual-currency sweepstakes casino model is now banned. Unlike operators that retrofitted an existing sweepstakes product, Albumza was designed around a single-currency framework from day one — which is exactly why it sits among the most defensible legal positions in this directory.

## Why we rank Albumza #1

Three things put Albumza at the top of our list:

1. **Purpose-built for the post-ban landscape.** It isn't a sweepstakes casino in disguise. The single-currency architecture is the product, not a workaround tacked on after the fact.
2. **Fast redemptions.** Cash-out is one of the most common pain points players bring up about ex-sweepstakes alternatives. Albumza runs an established payout flow that moves quickly once KYC is complete.
3. **Broad availability.** It works in both California and New York — the two largest banned markets — and in most of the other banned states. Few alternatives can say that.

## How the legal model works

State bans like California's AB 831 and New York's SB 5935 / AB 6745 target a specific mechanic: a **dual-currency** system in which a "free" coin and a redeemable "sweeps" coin run in parallel. Albumza uses a **single currency** — no parallel redeemable coin sitting alongside a play-money coin. That structural difference is what places it outside the scope of those statutes.

Single-currency products are newer than [parimutuel gaming](/guides/parimutuel-explained), which has decades of regulatory history. We still rate Albumza **low risk**, because the design is clean, the redemption mechanics are documented, and the platform has been operating in CA and NY rather than dodging those markets.

## California availability

Available. Albumza operates in California under its single-currency framework. AB 831 is written around the dual-currency mechanic, so a genuinely single-currency product is structured to sit outside it. Verify directly with the platform before depositing.

## Redemption experience

Because Albumza runs a single currency, redemption is a direct cash-out from your account balance rather than the two-step "purchase Gold Coins, redeem Sweeps Coins" dance of the banned model. Once you've completed KYC, withdrawals move quickly through the platform's standard payout flow.

## How to sign up

Standard flow: create an account, verify email and age, confirm your state, and complete KYC before your first cash-out.
