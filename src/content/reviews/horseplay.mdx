## What is Horseplay?

Horseplay is a real-money platform from Game Play Network that combines **parimutuel wagering** with a reveal-style game presentation. The underlying engine is pooled wagering, but the player-facing experience is designed to feel more like a game than like betting a race card.

## How the legal model works

Horseplay rests on the [parimutuel model](/guides/parimutuel-explained): players wager into a shared pool, and payouts come from that pool. Because it isn't a dual-currency sweepstakes product, the state bans don't reach it, and we rate it **low** risk.

One honest caveat: the reveal-style presentation can make it less obvious that you're participating in pooled wagering. That's a UX observation, not a legal concern, the model itself is sound.

## California availability

Fully available. California's parimutuel framework supports Horseplay, making it a strong low-risk option for California players.

## New York availability

Partial. Horseplay has some presence in New York, which is unusual for a low-risk platform, most either serve California only or pull out entirely. Confirm the current scope of New York access directly with the platform.

## Other states

Availability depends on each state's wagering laws. Check Horseplay for your specific state.

## How to sign up

Standard real-money flow: register, verify identity and age, fund the account, confirm location.

## Redemption experience

Winnings are real funds drawn from the wagering pool, withdrawn through the platform's standard payout and verification process.

## Our verdict

Horseplay is a strong pick for California players and one of the few low-risk options with any New York footprint. The reveal-style wrapper makes it more approachable than raw ADW. Just remember it's real-money pooled wagering underneath the game layer.
