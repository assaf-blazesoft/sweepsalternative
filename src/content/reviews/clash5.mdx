## What is Clash5?

Clash5 is a newer entrant positioning itself as a legal play option for players in banned states. Public information suggests it uses a **single-currency** model similar to [CardCrush](/review/cardcrush), but the details have not been independently confirmed.

## How the legal model works

In principle, a single-currency platform avoids the dual Gold Coin / Sweeps Coin mechanic that the state bans target. **In Clash5's case, we have not verified that the implementation actually works this way.** A platform can describe itself as single-currency while still operating in a legally grey manner, so the label alone is not enough for us to vouch for it.

This is why Clash5 carries an **unknown** risk rating and why we are not yet recommending it.

## California availability

Unconfirmed. We have not been able to verify whether Clash5 legally serves California players under AB 831. Do not treat its availability as settled.

## New York availability

Unconfirmed, for the same reasons. New York's ban is strict and recently enacted; assume nothing until Clash5 documents its position.

## Other states

Insufficient information to assess.

## How to sign up

We are deliberately not walking through a sign-up flow for a platform whose legal status we cannot confirm. If you choose to investigate Clash5, read its terms of service carefully and confirm it is licensed or lawfully operating in your state first.

## Our verdict

Clash5 is on our watch list, not our recommended list. The single-currency framing is encouraging, but encouraging is not the same as confirmed. We will update this review the moment we can verify how the model works and where it legally operates.
