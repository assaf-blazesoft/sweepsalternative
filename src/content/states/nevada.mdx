## Why sweepstakes casinos are restricted in Nevada

Nevada is the irony of this whole story: the most gaming-friendly state in America is one of the *least* friendly to dual-currency sweepstakes casinos. Nevada protects its licensed gaming industry fiercely, and its enforcement powers, including those under **SB256**, give regulators wide latitude against unlicensed operators. Most sweepstakes operators **self-excluded** from Nevada rather than risk action.

We classify Nevada as **restricted** (enforcement-based). See [what happened to sweepstakes casinos](/guides/what-happened).

## What to look for in an alternative

In Nevada, the licensed real-money gaming options are extensive, but for the specific online, play-from-home experience sweepstakes offered, the alternatives are the same model-based platforms we track. Confirm Nevada availability directly, and recognize that operators are cautious here for good reason.
