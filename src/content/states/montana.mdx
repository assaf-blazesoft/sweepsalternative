## Why sweepstakes casinos got banned in Montana

Montana's **SB 555** took effect in **October 2025**. Montana has long taken a conservative line on gambling, channeling most legal gaming through tightly defined state systems. Dual-currency sweepstakes casinos, which reached players directly online, didn't fit that model, and SB 555 made the prohibition explicit.

See [what happened to sweepstakes casinos](/guides/what-happened) for how Montana fits the national pattern.

## What to look for in an alternative

Montana's smaller market means fewer platforms actively target it, so availability is the key thing to verify. Don't assume a platform that works in California is live in Montana. Favor established legal models and confirm current Montana access before depositing.
