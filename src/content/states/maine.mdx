## Why sweepstakes casinos got banned in Maine

Maine's **LD 2007** takes effect **July 14, 2026**. Like Indiana, Maine is currently in a transition window, the ban is law but not yet in force. Maine's gaming market is small and tightly regulated around its tribal and casino frameworks, and dual-currency sweepstakes products fell outside all of it.

Expect operators to exit around the effective date. See [what happened to sweepstakes casinos](/guides/what-happened) for context.

## What to look for in an alternative

Maine players should prepare before the July 14 cutoff. Because Maine is a small market, availability varies more than in large states, verify that any alternative actually serves Maine, and prefer established legal models with the clearest footing.
