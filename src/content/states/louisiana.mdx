## Why sweepstakes casinos got banned in Louisiana

Louisiana's **HB 883** took effect in **May 2026**. Louisiana has a large, established gaming industry, riverboat casinos, land-based venues, and regulated sports betting, so the state has both strong commercial incentives and a regulatory apparatus geared toward licensed operators. Unlicensed dual-currency sweepstakes products were a direct competitive and legal mismatch, and HB 883 prohibited them.

This is one of the most recent bans. See [what happened to sweepstakes casinos](/guides/what-happened) for the national arc.

## What to look for in an alternative

Right after a ban, the gap between "still advertising" and "actually compliant" is widest. Confirm current Louisiana availability and legal model for any platform before depositing, and favor the established [parimutuel](/guides/parimutuel-explained) and DFS options.
