## Why sweepstakes casinos are restricted in Michigan

Michigan took the **enforcement** route. The **Michigan Gaming Control Board (MGCB)** moved against dual-currency sweepstakes operators using its existing regulatory authority rather than waiting for new legislation. Michigan runs one of the most successful regulated online casino and sports betting markets in the country, and it has a clear institutional interest in protecting that licensed framework from unlicensed competitors.

We classify Michigan as **restricted** (enforcement-based). See [what happened to sweepstakes casinos](/guides/what-happened).

## What to look for in an alternative

Michigan's active regulator means compliant operators take the rules seriously, which is good for you, but it also means anything still pushing a dual-currency product is a clear warning sign. Favor established legal models and confirm current Michigan availability before signing up.
