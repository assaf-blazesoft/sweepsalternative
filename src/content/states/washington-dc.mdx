## Why sweepstakes casinos are at risk in Washington DC

Washington DC hasn't banned dual-currency sweepstakes casinos yet, but it's moving in that direction. **Council Bill 26-0656** had its **first hearing in May 2026**, putting DC firmly on the watch list. We classify it as **at risk** rather than banned.

If the bill advances on the pattern we've seen elsewhere, DC players should expect operators to begin exiting once a ban is enacted and dated. This is a good moment to understand your alternatives *before* a cutoff arrives. See [what happened to sweepstakes casinos](/guides/what-happened).

## What to look for in an alternative

Because DC's status is in flux, the smart move is to identify alternatives now rather than wait. Favor platforms on established legal models that don't depend on the sweepstakes structure at all, those won't be affected if and when a DC ban passes. Re-check this page as the bill progresses.
