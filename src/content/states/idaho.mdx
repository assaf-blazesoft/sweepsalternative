## Why sweepstakes casinos are restricted in Idaho

Idaho's restriction is **constitutional**, not a recent statute. The state constitution prohibits casino gambling and specifically bars simulations of casino games, and Idaho does not allow the cash-redemption mechanism that defines the sweepstakes model. This is one of the most categorical anti-gambling postures in the country, and it long predates dual-currency sweepstakes casinos.

As a result, the model never had real room to operate in Idaho. We classify the state as **restricted** (enforcement of pre-existing law). See [what happened to sweepstakes casinos](/guides/what-happened).

## What to look for in an alternative

Idaho's constitutional ban on casino *simulations* is broad, which narrows the field considerably. Be conservative: verify any platform's Idaho status carefully, and lean toward models that are clearly not casino simulations, such as [peer-to-peer DFS](/guides/dfs-vs-casino), while confirming current availability.
