## Why sweepstakes casinos got banned in Connecticut

Connecticut's **SB 1235** took effect in **June 2025**. Connecticut runs a tightly controlled gaming market, online gambling is limited to a small number of tribal-affiliated operators, so unlicensed dual-currency sweepstakes products sat squarely outside the state's carefully negotiated framework. The ban closed that gap.

For the national timeline, see [what happened to sweepstakes casinos](/guides/what-happened).

## What to look for in an alternative

In a tightly regulated market like Connecticut, the gap between "legal alternative" and "operating in a grey area" matters more than usual. Stick to platforms with a clearly different legal model, [parimutuel](/guides/parimutuel-explained) options are the firmest footing, and confirm current Connecticut availability before signing up.
